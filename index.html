Tuna Salad

The Best Healthy Tuna Salad! Anyone else love a good tuna salad? It is one of my favorite recipes to make for a quick, easy, and satisfying lunch. Tuna is packed with protein and when you add a little mayonnaise to the mix you also include a great source of healthy fats. This healthy tuna salad is made with fresh veggies,and spice to add incredible flavor and take your tuna salad to the next level.

Ingredients

    Canned Tuna: In Water
    Celery
    Cucumber
    Lime Juice
    Mayonnaise
    Pink Himalayan Salt
    Black Pepper
