Tuna Salad Receipe

The Best Healthy Tuna Salad! Anyone else love a good tuna salad? It is one of my favorite recipes to make for a quick, easy, and satisfying lunch. Tuna is packed with protein and when you add a little mayonnaise to the mix you also include a great source of healthy fats. This healthy tuna salad is made with fresh veggies,and spice to add incredible flavor and take your tuna salad to the next level.

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna: In Water
  • Celery
  • Cucumber
  • Lime Juice
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pink Himalayan Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Crackers

    • Steps

    1. Drain the cans or pouches of tuna. I like to press the lid of the can against the tuna to ensure that all of the water has been removed. Then transfer it into a bowl and use a fork to separate the pieces.
    2. Chop up your celery and cucumbers to small pieces.
    3. Next, add a small cap size of lemon juice to the tune. Then add the mayonnaise, celery, cumcumber, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
    4. Stir everything until well combined and give the tuna salad a taste. Adjust the levels of salt and pepper to fit your tastebuds and then either serve immediately or place the tuna salad in an air-tight container in the fridge.

    How to Serve Tuna Salad?

    There are several different ways you can serve this healthy tuna salad.

    Here are a few ideas:

  • Pair it with crackers and fresh veggies
  • Make a tuna salad sandwich on your favorite toasted bread
  • Scoop it into lettuce wraps
